code for palestine is a project that is implemented by the paltel group foundation and will be held in cooperation with jawwal. it aims to teach students in the fields of technology and design thinking where they will be given access to the world of high tech, coding, business and leadership skills. student who are selected will be entering the 10th grade school year or the age of 14 years old and will continue with the project gram for duration of 3 years. the summer camp will be held in the west bank town of jericho at paltel group club and training center, and in gaza strip at al-Mat'haf hotel & culture house. the 2017 program will commence on july 14th, 2017 and conclude on july 28th 2017. everyday will begin at 8:00am sharp and end around 5:00pm, depending on the instructions provided by the instructors
